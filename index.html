    What is CyberRely?

    CyberRely is a free software that aims to provide free cyber-protection against cyber-crime acts. It contains of 4 services: Phishsafe, VirusSafe, MailSafe and an unique Encryption technique.

    Phishsafe is a automated phishing detecting software. Using machine learning and a lot of inputs, we finally achived 96% accuracy.

    VirusSafe  is a virus detecting software which utilises the checksum algorithm to deliver you with the most accurate results and keep your PC safe.

    Mailsafe is a automated spam detection algorithm, using neural-networds,  that helps your mail-box be spam-free. And don't worry, if you ever need  that mail, just check the spam folder.

    The Encryption technique uses a complex 3-tier algorithm that helps to keep the file safe from unwanted-readers. The first layer of the file is encrypted using a algorithm similar to the md5 function. Then, the second and the third layer is enrypted by our own devised algorithm we like to call cpg technique.

    With all 4 of the serveices in your PC, you, your PC and your perosnal data ,all will be safe-guarded.
